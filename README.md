# Generative Research Methods

> Part of my [portfolio](https://github.com/Spendry/portfolio).

A curriculum for framework engineering, published together with the practice manual its first term trains from.

| Document | What it is |
|---|---|
| [Generative Research Methods: A Curriculum for Framework Engineering](generative-research-methods-curriculum.md) | A five-term course of study built on five axioms: generate abundantly, store contradictions without panic, write the myth before the math, pre-register every kill condition, and keep the ledger public. Includes a sample Term 3 syllabus, the register discipline specification, and the Kill Condition Protocol. |
| [The Node-Space Navigation Guide](node-space-navigation-guide.md) | The Term 1 practice manual: a method for pre-linguistic synthesis, with a five-week starting protocol, navigation strategies, space-sculpting exercises, and the Three-Breath Method for synthesis under pressure. |

Start with the curriculum's five axioms; the guide is their operating instructions.

The documents reference frameworks not yet published (Boundary-Native Set Theory, Compositional Intelligence Theory, the Mediation Paradox). Both stand on their own; the referenced releases will get their own repos.
